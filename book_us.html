Book Us At Your Event

Denver Metro Jazz is an 18-piece non profit big band dedicated to making live jazz music accessible to the community. Our mission is to bring the energy and excitement of big band music to local events at little to no cost for the public. We work with community organizations, event planners, and nonprofits to provide high-quality performances at flexible pricing, ensuring live jazz remains a vibrant part of Denver’s cultural scene.

We invite you to experience the dynamic energy of Denver Metro Jazz at your next event. Whether you're hosting a corporate gathering, a private celebration, or a community function, our ensemble is ready to deliver a captivating performance that will leave a lasting impression on your guests. With a focus on creating memorable experiences, we tailor our sets to fit the unique atmosphere of your venue. Let us bring the timeless charm of big band jazz to your event—contact us today to discuss how we can make your occasion truly unforgettable!

Email, text or call us to discuss performance pricing and scheduling. We look forward to collaborating with you to bring big band flair to your event.
