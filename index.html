Welcome to

Denver Metro Jazz

Denver's premier community big band-bringing live jazz to public spaces, festivals, and local events. Built by and for the community, Denver Metro Jazz features local musicians at local venues with the aim of providing the people of Colorado accessible and professional caliber big band music!


Our Mission

We strive to bring high-quality, accessible jazz to Denver's communities, offering engaging performances that connect and inspire audiences. Through our work, we aim to enrich Denver's cultural landscape and keep the idiom of jazz thriving in our cities.

Our Music

Our repertoire includes a diverse range of music, from the timeless classics of jazz legends to iconic pop hits reimagined through the big band sound. Our selections also includes new arrangements from talented local artists, showcasing the next generation of the genre.

Our Members

Our musicians are dedicated, non-occupational musicians from the greater Denver area, committed to excellence and fostering a culture of growth, collaboration, and professionalism in every performance.
